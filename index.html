About Us


Redolence Hotels & Resorts - Welcome to Redolence Hotels & Resorts, a brand that is dedicated to delivering a luxurious and comfortable experience to our guests.


Redolence Hotels & Resorts was founded with the goal of providing travelers with a sense of home away from home. We believe that a hotel should be more than just a place to sleep - it should be a place to relax, unwind, and make memories.


Redolence Aravali Tree - Welcome to Redolence Aravali Tree. Located near the epicentre of Udaipur, a city rich in culture and history, Redolence Aravali Tree has been serving guests for many years and has become a beloved destination for travelers from all over the world.
